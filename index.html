Maison Felix Salut
Felix Salut (formerly known as Felix Weigand) is an Amsterdam-based designer. He runs his own design label and studio that bridges the fields of typography, design, fashion, and visual art. Contact: hello@felixsalut.com

Jaehyun Kim completed an internship at Felix Salut’s studio from 1 September to 21 November.

The working schedule was flexible, typically consisting of 4–5 days per week. Jaehyun worked at the studio from Monday to Thursday, 10:00–18:00, and attended Felix’s Typography class at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie on Fridays from 9:30–12:30. Wednesdays were often taken off or used as remote-working day.

Location: Amsterdam, NL.
